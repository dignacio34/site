Theatre


News and Upcoming Projects:

This summer, I will be making my DC theatre debut! I will be in the ensemble of La Ti Do Productions' Soul Redeemer, directed by Anya Randall Nebel and written by Paul Handy and Neal Learner! It will be part of the Capital Fringe Festival and performances will be on July 9, 14, 20, 21, 25, and 27 in the Southwest DC area.

I will also be interning with Keegan Theatre while in DC this summer! I'll be working with Keegan's Play-Rah-Ka education program and I'll also work with their PR department to develop content and PR strategies.

I'll be performing in Caleen Sinnette Jennings' final play at AU, Shared Space on October 3-6 in the Katzen Studio Theatre.
