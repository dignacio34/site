Journalism


My love for storytelling extends beyond the stage, and onto the page. I absolutely love journalism, writing, and multimedia, because it's an incredible way to tell others' stories and give them a platform to be heard. My main focus as a journalist is arts and cultural criticism, feature writing, news writing, and copy editing. I’m also very interested in issues relating to outreach, diversity, representation, and education, and am interested in combining my background in multimedia and reporting with my theatre work.  On this page, I have included some of my favorite articles I've written!
